What I do

I have more than 7 years' experience working for B2B companies in various marketing roles. Below is a quick overview of the skills I have developed and utlize in my professional career.

Market research

Whether it's interviewing customers, combing through data to discover trends, or scoping out competitor's booths at trade shows, my market research skills have improved customer experience, segmentation, and processes.

Adobe Indesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop

A skillset that came out of a need to design my own marketing materials, I have substantial experience using Indesign, Illustrator, and Photoshop. Being able to utilize this skillset to become a versatile team member makes me proud.

Marketing Automation

Reaching prospects and customers in a timely manner is crucial to stay top of mind. I've used multiple platforms and built robust workflows with a long term vision to keep my current customers engaged and nurture prospects into customers

CRMs

Understanding what customers are doing and being able to pull out actionable insights is important for any marketer. I have experience with a range of CRMs, from simple-easy-to-use (Salesforce) to more complex (SAP CRM).

HTML & CSS

What started as a high school hobby has turned into a valuable skill in my professional career. My front-end development skills has built many emails from scratch, customized landing pages, and built my personal website.

Microsoft Office

Using Excel to quickly dissect data, present it in PowerPoint that speaks to the audience, and managing projects in Planner are all skills I'm constantly improving year after year. I enjoy finding new tricks to make my job just a little bit easier.
