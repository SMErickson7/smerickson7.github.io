About Me

For over years, I have combined strategic marketing with technical enablement to drive product success. My work is guided by a central mission: making technology work for people, not the other way around. I believe the best products don't just "function" but rather they feel intuitive, solve real problems, and empower the teams that use them.

Bridging the gap between technical capability and human adoption.

My background in B2B marketing taught me that data tells you what is happening, but understanding user behavior tells you why. I leveraged this curiosity to pivot from traditional campaign marketing to the product side of the business. I found that true "digital transformation" isn't about buying new software, it's about deeply understanding the user's journey and engineering processes that respect their time and intelligence.

Today, as a Digital Enablement Manager, I focus on the intersection of Product, People, and Process. Whether it's re-engineering an e-commerce platform or automating internal workflows, I translate complex technical requirements into seamless user experiences. I partner with cross-functional teams to drive digital adoption, ensuring that technology actually delivers on its promise for both customers and employees.
